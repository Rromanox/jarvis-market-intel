My Understanding: Institutional crypto flows don't directly correlate with prices in the short term. XRP saw +500% institutional inflows while dropping 60%, Solana had 1000%+ institutional interest while falling 79%. Key Insight: Only 4 cryptocurrencies have regulated institutional products (Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, Solana), creating liquidity concentration. How It Applies: I can track institutional flows independently of price action to identify medium-term opportunities. When flows and price diverge (high institutional interest + low prices), it creates optimal entry points. I need to separate my institutional flow analysis from my technical price analysis completely.
